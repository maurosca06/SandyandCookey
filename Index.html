Our Story

Sandy and Cookey started as a dream shared by a group of close friends who shared a deep, unconditional love for good food. We wanted to create a cozy place where people could enjoy simple, comforting, yet high-quality meals.

Located beautifully in Santa Teresita, we bake our cookies throughout the day so they are always warm, and we assemble our sandwiches with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. For us, this isn't just a business; it's our way of sharing joy with our community, one sandwich and one cookie at a time.
